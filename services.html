What We Do

Parsec Lab provides complete datacenter and campus MEP engineering services. From concept through commissioning, we design resilient mechanical, electrical, and controls systems for mission-critical facilities. We also design and produce innovative cooling equipment purpose-built for high-density compute environments.

We partner with enterprises, hyperscalers, and colocation providers to turn concepts into operational assets. Whether it's a greenfield datacenter campus or a brownfield retrofit, our approach is collaborative, technically rigorous, and informed by real-world engineering experience.

End-to-End MEP Design

Full-scope mechanical, electrical, and plumbing design for datacenter facilities and campus infrastructure. From site master planning to as-built documentation, our deliverables ensure technical rigor and code compliance.

Cooling Equipment Design & Production

We design and manufacture innovative cooling solutions including custom air handling units, rear-door heat exchangers, and liquid cooling distribution systems optimized for high-density compute racks.

Scalable Infrastructure

Whether it's a 1 MW edge site or a 100 MW hyperscale campus, our modular design frameworks allow rapid deployment and seamless capacity scaling with minimal rework.

Mission-Critical Systems Integration

Our engineers specialize in integrating complex power, cooling, and control systems into unified, fault-tolerant architectures. We work across Tier I-IV environments for optimal reliability.

  • Redundant UPS & generator systems
  • Hot & cold aisle containment
  • Automated building & power management
  • Fire detection & suppression design
  • Direct liquid cooling integration

Project Delivery & Support

We go beyond design by supporting execution and operations throughout the facility lifecycle:

  • Construction administration & commissioning
  • Regulatory approvals and compliance documentation
  • Ongoing performance analysis & infrastructure audits
  • Capacity planning for IT expansion
  • Campus MEP coordination across multiple buildings

Datacenter & Campus MEP Design

Parsec Lab provides holistic MEP engineering for datacenter facilities and surrounding campus infrastructure. Our team integrates mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and controls disciplines from day one, ensuring coordinated designs that minimize change orders and accelerate construction timelines.

Power & Cooling Systems

Uptime starts with power and cooling done right. Our electrical engineers model every scenario from dual-feed redundancy to generator run-time analysis. On the mechanical side, we design precision cooling systems including chilled water plants, economizer strategies, and direct liquid cooling for AI/HPC workloads.

Innovative Cooling Equipment

Beyond design services, Parsec Lab engineers and produces custom cooling equipment. Our in-house team develops purpose-built heat exchangers, coolant distribution units, and modular cooling systems designed for the extreme demands of modern high-density datacenter environments. From prototype to production, we control every step.

BIM Coordination

Our BIM workflows drive clash-free construction, faster installations, and real-time cost control. Every rack, cable tray, and cooling unit is modeled with purpose. We maintain digital twins that support both construction and ongoing facility management throughout the asset lifecycle.

Security & Compliance

Physical security, regulatory compliance, and fire safety are engineered into every facility. From access control and compartmentalized fire zones to full code compliance documentation, we design systems that protect both people and critical infrastructure.

Smart Infrastructure & Remote Ops

We integrate BMS, DCIM, and custom telemetry into a unified platform. Whether you're on-site or remote, our dashboards provide real-time performance data, alerts, and trend analysis. Controls engineering is at the core of every smart infrastructure solution we deliver.
