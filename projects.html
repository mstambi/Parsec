Hyperscale Template Design — AMER & EMEA

Template design development for flexible air-cooled and liquid-cooled hyperscale data centers exceeding 60 MW, deployable across AMER and EMEA regions. Designed for rapid scalability with configurable power and cooling blocks.

Technical Specifications

Total IT Capacity 88 MW (Two 44 MW data halls)
Block Size 2.75 MW configurable blocks
Power 100% UPS and generator backed
Max Rack Power Up to 2.75 MW per position
Cooling Air 0–100%, Liquid 0–100%, configurable
Cooling Inlet 27°C liquid, ambient 10–35°C air
Annualized PUE < 1.15
WUE 0.4 L/kWh
