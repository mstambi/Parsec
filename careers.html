Careers

Build the Future of Data Center Infrastructure

WHY PARSEC LAB

More Than a Job — A Mission

At Parsec Lab, engineers work on cutting-edge data center projects that power the world's most demanding compute environments. Our team collaborates across disciplines — architecture, mechanical, electrical, controls — on projects spanning three continents.

Based at ZICER (Zagreb Innovation Centre), we combine the energy of a startup with the technical depth of world-class infrastructure engineering. Here, you won't just design systems — you'll shape the future of critical infrastructure.

International Projects

Work on hyperscale facilities across USA, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

All Disciplines

Collaborate with architects, mechanical, electrical, and controls engineers daily.

Innovation Focus

Contribute to custom cooling equipment development, not just design services.

Growth Environment

ZICER-based startup with mentorship from industry veterans and rapid career growth.

What We Value

Technical Excellence

Deep expertise in your discipline. We value engineers who continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in critical infrastructure design.

Collaborative Spirit

Data center projects require tight coordination. We need people who thrive in multidisciplinary teams and communicate clearly across trades.

Global Mindset

Our projects span continents and codes. We seek engineers comfortable with international standards from ASHRAE to EN 50600.
