About Us

Engineering Excellence, Every Discipline

OUR STORY

Founded on International Experience

Parsec Lab was founded in late 2024 in Zagreb, Croatia, by a team of engineers with extensive international experience in data center design and critical infrastructure. Our founders recognized that the industry needed a firm capable of leading projects across every engineering discipline — not just MEP, but architecture, controls, structural, and equipment development.

Based at ZICER (Zagreb Innovation Centre), we combine startup agility with the deep technical expertise of engineers who have delivered projects across the United States, Europe, Middle East, and Asia-Pacific regions.

Our capabilities span the full lifecycle of data center infrastructure, including the development of specialized cooling systems, power distribution solutions, and intelligent controls platforms.

HOW WE WORK

Integrated Engineering Under One Roof

Multidisciplinary

Architects, mechanical, electrical, and controls engineers working side by side from project kickoff. No silos, no coordination gaps.

Global Standards

Experienced with ASHRAE, EN 50600, Uptime Institute, and regional codes across AMER, EMEA, and APAC. We design for your jurisdiction.

Design + Manufacture

We don't just draw — we build. Our team engineers and produces custom cooling equipment, bridging the gap between design intent and physical hardware.
