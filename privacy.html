Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how Parsec Lab d.o.o., located at ZICER, Zagreb Innovation Centre, Avenija Dubrovnik 15, 10000 Zagreb, Croatia ("Parsec Lab", "we", "our" or "us"), collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data of visitors and users ("you") of our website.

1. Data We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide via forms or communication.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, referral source, and browsing behavior on our site.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. See our Cookie Policy for more.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and provide our services
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our site
  • To analyze usage and performance of the website
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests

4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist us with web hosting, analytics, or communications
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable laws
  • International data transfers will only occur with adequate safeguards as required by GDPR

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority (AZOP in Croatia)

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The updated version will be posted on our website with the effective date.
