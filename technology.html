OUR APPROACH

From Design to Production, Under One Roof

Parsec Lab doesn't just design cooling systems — we engineer and manufacture custom equipment. Our team develops purpose-built heat exchangers, coolant distribution units, air handling units, and modular cooling systems for the extreme demands of modern high-density data centers.

From initial concept and thermal simulation through prototype testing to full production, every step is controlled in-house. This integrated approach ensures tight quality control, faster iteration cycles, and equipment that precisely matches the performance requirements of each deployment.

PRODUCT PORTFOLIO

Engineered for High-Density Environments

Custom Air Handling Units

150 kW to 300 kW cooling capacity

Purpose-designed AHUs for data center environments across EMEA. Engineered for high airflow efficiency, easy maintenance access, and integration with indirect evaporative cooling strategies. Configurable for both recirculation and mixed-air modes.

  • Cooling capacity 150–300 kW
  • ISO 14644-1 Class 8 air quality
  • ANSI/ISA-71.04-2013 gaseous compliance

Coolant Distribution Units (CDUs)

Precision liquid cooling for AI/HPC

Precision coolant distribution for direct liquid cooling deployments. Designed to support AI/HPC workloads with inlet temperatures of 27°C and flow rates exceeding 1.5 lpm/kW. Integrated leak monitoring and automated flow balancing.

  • 27°C inlet temperature
  • >1.5 lpm/kW flow rate
  • Continuous leak monitoring

Heat Exchangers

Rear-door and in-row configurations

Custom rear-door and in-row heat exchangers optimized for high-density rack configurations. Engineered for racks ranging from standard 600mm to custom 2-foot to 20-foot configurations with floor loading capacity up to 5,000 lbs.

  • Supports rack sizes from 2 ft to 20 ft
  • Floor loading up to 5,000 lbs
  • Modular mounting options

Modular Cooling Systems

Scalable from 500 kW to multi-MW

Scalable, pre-engineered cooling modules for rapid deployment. Designed to support data center configurations from 500 kW to multi-MW facilities. Air-to-liquid cooling split is configurable up to 15 months before IST and can be reconfigured over the facility's lifetime.

  • Scalable from 500 kW
  • Flexible air/liquid split
  • Reconfigurable over facility life
